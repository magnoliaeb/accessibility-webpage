Eye Click

EyeClick is an application for people with cerebral palsy that can't perform a click with the conventional mouse or accessibility button. With this program and a conventional webcam, a left click is performed when the user closes both eyes.

Face Mouse

FaceMouse is an application that allows you to move the screen cursor with your head, aimed to people with psychomotor disabilities.
